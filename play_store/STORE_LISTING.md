## Data safety form  (App content → Data safety)
Answer to match the privacy policy:

- **Does your app collect or share user data?** → Yes
- **Data type:** *App activity / Other user-generated content* → the text the user chooses to correct
- **Collected or shared?** → **Shared** (sent to AI provider for processing) and processed in transit
- **Is it processed ephemerally?** → **Yes** — not stored on the server after the request
- **Is data encrypted in transit?** → **Yes** (HTTPS)
- **Can users request deletion?** → Not stored, so nothing to delete
- **Is data sold?** → **No**
- **Purpose:** App functionality (grammar correction / suggestions)

> ⚠️ Keyboard apps get extra review. Be accurate here and make sure the privacy
> policy says the same thing (it does — `docs/privacy-policy.html`).

## Content rating  (App content → Content rating)
- Category: *Utility / Productivity*
- No violence, no mature content, no user-to-user communication → expected rating **Everyone / 3+**

## Target audience
- Target age: **18+** (or 13+) — **not** designed for children.
